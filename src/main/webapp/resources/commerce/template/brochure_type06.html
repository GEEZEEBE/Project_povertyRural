WELCOME TO MALAYSIA
MALAYSIA IS
Malaysia is a federal constitutional monarchy in Southeast Asia. It consists of thirteen states and three federal territories and has a total landmass of 329,847 square kilmetres (127,350 sq mi) separated by the South China Sea into two similarly sized rgions, Peninsular Malaysia and Malaysian Borneo.
